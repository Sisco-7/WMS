Our Facilities

At Winsome Model Schools, we pride ourselves on providing state-of-the-art facilities that enhance the learning experience. From advanced laboratories to expansive sports complexes, our infrastructure is designed to support both academic and extracurricular growth.

Explore our facilities below, take a virtual tour, and even book a space for events or activities.

Smart Classrooms

Equipped with interactive smartboards, projectors, and ergonomic seating for an immersive learning experience.

Capacity: 30 students per classroom

Features: High-speed Wi-Fi, air conditioning, digital resources

Science Laboratories

Advanced labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with modern equipment and safety protocols.

Capacity: 25 students per lab

Features: Fume hoods, microscopes, chemical storage

Library

A vast collection of over 10,000 books, e-books, journals, and digital resources.

Capacity: 100 students

Features: Quiet study areas, computer stations, online access

Sports Complex

Includes a football field, basketball court, swimming pool, and indoor gym.

Capacity: 500 spectators

Features: Changing rooms, equipment storage, floodlights

ICT Center

State-of-the-art computer labs with high-speed internet and the latest software.

Capacity: 40 students

Features: Coding stations, VR setups, 3D printers

Auditorium

A 500-seat auditorium for events, performances, and assemblies.

Features: Stage lighting, sound system, projector

Availability: Bookable for events

Facility Usage Guidelines

  • Users must adhere to safety protocols and clean up after use.
  • Damages to facilities will incur a fine based on repair costs.
  • Cancellations must be made 48 hours prior to the booking date.
